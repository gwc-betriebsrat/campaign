How to Vote & FAQ

Your participation is crucial! Here is all the information you need to support Global Workforce Community.

Casting Your Vote

Election Period: 5 – 8 May 2026

Voting Method: Voting will be conducted via a physical ballot. You will have the option to either vote in person at the office or vote by post. The Electoral Board will send detailed instructions to all eligible employees.

On the physical ballot paper you receive, please select:
List 3: Global Workforce Community

What is a Nomination List?
It's a document with a list of candidates running together. To be valid, each list requires at least 50 employee signatures. The election is based on voting for these different Nomination Lists.
If I sign a list to support it, does that count as my vote?
No. Signing a list is only to help get it on the ballot. You are still completely free to vote for any list you choose during the election period. Your supporting signature is only visible to the candidates on that list and the Electoral Board; it is not made public.
How many Nomination Lists can I sign as a supporter?
You are only allowed to sign one. If you sign more than one, the Electoral Board will ask you to choose one to support.
How can I get in touch with questions?
The best way is to post your question in our zLife group or contact any of our candidates directly. We are always happy to hear from you!
