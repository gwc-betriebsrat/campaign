Who We Are — Candidate Card Concepts

Seven different ways to show candidate introductions without sending people to a separate article. All use real bios. Pick the one you like and we'll build it out for the full page.

Concept A
Tooltip on hover
Hover over any card to read the person's intro in a dark popover above it. No click needed.
