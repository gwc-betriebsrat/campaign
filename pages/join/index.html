Works Council Election 2026
Your Vote is Your Voice.
This is your chance to stop watching from the sidelines and take an active role in shaping your workplace. When election day comes — vote for GWC.
How to vote

Voting takes place onsite by default. You'll receive a physical ballot paper — find GWC on the list and make your mark.

Can't make it in?

On long-term absence or working remotely that day? Proactively request a postal ballot from the Electoral Board — don't wait, deadlines are strict.

Why it matters

A vote for GWC is a vote for people who know this workplace from the inside — colleagues who will use that knowledge to co-create policies that reflect your daily reality.
